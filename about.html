Who am I?

About Me

My name is Brandon Chen. I'm currently a student at SAIT, learning about Graphic Design. "diaL" is the online username that I go by, in which I show off my skills & designs. My work is mostly inspired by modern streetwear style graphics, with a pinch of "Y2K" style. I'm a graphic designer, but I'm also looking to branch out and improve my skills in other aspects like music or coding.

What I do

From Graphic Design, Logo Design, to Photo Manipulation & Composites. I'm versed in After Effects, a well as Premiere, and have edited videos for myself before. I also dabble in some photography. While I am an amateur, I've been improving my skills, and when compared with previous works from years back, the changes are definitely noticable.

Programs I use

For graphic designs, my go-to programs consist of Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom, and as of right now, Canva (The online editing program). For things like music, I've been dabbling in BandLab, but other than that I have yet to try other programs related to music. For Editing, I use Adobe Premiere. As for animation, I've only used Adobe After Effects. Outside of graphic designs, I occasionally do digital art on Clip Studio Paint.
